ARPA-H Cohort

ARPA-H cohort included indiviuals with an exisiting record in STARR-OMOP and in the cancer registry (Neural Frame) or a present encounter of tumor board.

From the entire OMOP population there were 200,262 indiviuals who were eligible for ARPA-H cohort.

  • Neural Frame: Defined as patients within the Neural Frame who are permitted for research purposes (189k)

  • Tumor Board Encounter: Defined using EPIC patient encounter data (23k)
