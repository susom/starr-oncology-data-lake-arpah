OMOP Episode Table Metrics

Summary

The Episode table in OMOP Common Data Model (CDM) captures detailed information about clinical episodes representing treatment plans for patients in the oncology cohort. This table enables analysis of care trajectories and treatment patterns by providing granular episode-level data including episode start and end dates, episode types, and associated clinical events.

As of May 2026, this table includes 61,835 unique patients with 218,029 episodes IDs. Episodes span from 2002-09-02 to 2026-05-16.

Please note that episode records are more prevalent in recent years, which may reflect improvements in data capture and recording practices over time.
